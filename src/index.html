About me

Joe has built his career at the intersection of data science and academia. With a Master of Public Policy from Pepperdine University and a distinguished professorship at a Hispanic-serving institution, he has a proven track record in both academic leadership and hands-on technical work. His experience includes leading data science initiatives at a dynamic start-up, where he engineered databases, scripted ETL workflows, and developed algorithms that delivered measurable results for Fortune 500 companies. For the past eight years, Joe has been at the forefront of global data strategy with MicroStrategy, an enterprise leader in BI and AI. He has traveled extensively, deploying data-driven solutions that enable organizations to accelerate decision-making and optimize performance through advanced analytics. Most recently, Joe earned an Artificial Intelligence credential from MIT, marking the first step in his pivot toward medicine. He is committed to integrating AI, data science, and healthcare, with the goal of improving patient outcomes and operational efficiency in the medical field. As he pursues pre-medical education, Joe remains engaged in the data and analytics space, seeking to merge technology and education to deliver impactful solutions at the human level.
